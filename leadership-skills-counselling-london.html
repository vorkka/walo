Psychological Therapy & Counselling in London 

Offering a wide range of services geared to enhance your emotional wellbeing, professional development, leadership skills and life circumstances.

Psychological Therapy 

Dr Harrison is highly skilled and trained to provide psychological therapy that is supported by sound clinical research as being effective in helping you work through your personal difficulties. This includes the use of cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT), mindfulness therapy and acceptance & commitment therapy (ACT).

Professional Development & Tech Startup's 

Seeing a psychologist isn't always about dealing with problems. Many clients come in to further their professional development, self-development, leadership skills, decision making, public speaking and coping skills. Cognitive coaching utilises psychological knowledge to help you connect with your personal strengths and goals and ultimately and achieve your best. 

> Read More

 

Medico-Legal Reporting

Dr Harrison has experience and expertise in conducting clinical interviews and writing court reports in order to assist in legal proceedings. Medico-legal reports often help to clarify clinical questions about a person's particular struggles and provide new insights into complicated scenarios.   

 

Clinical Supervision

Sound and supportive supervision is key to fully developing clinical skills for any psychologist, therapist or counsellor. Dr Harrison provides ongoing clinical supervision, integrating multiple types of therapeutic approaches including CBT, Mindfulness, ACT and psychodynamic therapy. A main focus is helping supervisee's to explore, experiment and cultivate their own style.
